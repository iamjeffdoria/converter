Frequently asked
Do credits expire?▾
No — credits never expire. Buy once and use them at your own pace, whether that takes a week or a year.
What payment methods are accepted?▾
GCash, Maya (PayMaya), and major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard). More payment options may be added in the future.
What happens if an export fails?▾
If an export fails due to a server error on our end, the credits are automatically refunded to your account. You are never charged for failed exports.
Is there a refund policy?▾
If you purchased credits but haven't used any, contact us within 7 days for a full refund. Partially used packs are non-refundable.
Are my videos private?▾
Yes. Uploaded files are processed on our server and automatically deleted after 1 hour. We never store, share, or access your video content.
Can I buy more credits anytime?▾
Yes, you can buy any pack at any time and credits will be added to your existing balance. There's no limit on how many packs you can purchase.
Which platforms are supported?▾
YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Twitter/X, Discord, LinkedIn, and Facebook. Each platform has a one-click preset that sets the right resolution, quality, and file size automatically.
